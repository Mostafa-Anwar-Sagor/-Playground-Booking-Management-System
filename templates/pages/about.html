About Playground Booking

Revolutionizing how sports facilities connect with players and communities. We bridge the gap between playground owners and sports enthusiasts, creating seamless booking experiences.

Meet Our Founder

The visionary behind Playground Booking

MOSTAFA ANWAR

Founder & CEO

Mostafa Anwar is a passionate entrepreneur and sports enthusiast who founded Playground Booking with a vision to transform how people access and manage sports facilities. With a background in technology and a deep understanding of the sports industry, Mostafa recognized the need for a comprehensive platform that benefits both facility owners and players.

"I believe sports bring communities together and create lasting positive impact. My goal with Playground Booking is to make quality sports facilities accessible to everyone while empowering owners with the tools they need to succeed," says Mostafa.

Under his leadership, Playground Booking has grown to serve thousands of users across multiple countries, connecting sports communities and helping facility owners maximize their potential.
